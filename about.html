About Us

We have become experts in making scent marketing widespread and affordable

Founded in 2013, Sakāw started with three engineering students talking about a new type of marketing - scent marketing.

We discovered that companies use scents to create an inviting ambiance for their clients, and this was revolutionary to us. Our curiosity then took us one step further, and we started designing what a high performance and efficient diffusion system would look like.

Our success

Fast-forward to 2021, we are now headquartered in Montreal with an established production of our diffusion systems.

Through these years, we have developed countless fragrances thanks to our partnership with perfume houses from France that have provided the highest quality scents for our customers.

Our vision

Sakāw aims to become the leader in scent marketing and diffusion technology in North America with a broad range of cutting-edge products.
