Scent Marketing: the new way to reach clients

In a world where consumers are overloaded with marketing, fragrance diffusion is an innovative strategy to develop a subconscious brand loyalty. Scent marketing is more than a pleasant fragrance, it is a way to enhance your clients experience. The right fragrance will reinforce brand identity which will translate into an increase in sales.

The sense of smell is the most powerful of all senses due to its ability to unlock memories and create strong emotional connections called the Proust Phenomenon. Olfactory information has unique and direct pathways to the structures of the brain that deal with memories and emotions. This provides a strong link between smells and pleasant experiences, unlike other senses.
